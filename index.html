What's on my steel? 30-second diagnosis

Treatment Guide community-vetted

Orange dots after rainUsually rail-dust contamination, not pitting
First-line removalCitriSurf 77 / citric-acid passivation gel
Stubborn spotsBar Keepers Friend — light pressure, with grain
FingerprintsIsopropyl 50/50, microfiber
After any abrasiveRe-passivate: clean + 24h dry air

Do / Don't

  • Wash promptly after rain, road salt, bird droppings, bug splatter
  • Work WITH the brushed grain direction, always
  • Test any product on the lower rocker first
  • No chlorine / bleach cleaners — chloride pitting is permanent
  • No magnets on panels — galvanic marking
  • No aggressive buffing — strips the passivation layer
